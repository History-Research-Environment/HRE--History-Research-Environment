Convert a TMG Name Style to HRE

This screen allows you to convert an imported TMG Name Style to HRE format.

Elements of the selected TMG style are shown in the central column of the screen.

The right-hand column shows the equivalent HRE elements.

By selecting elements of the TMG list you may replace an HRE element with an element chosen from the 'All Elements' list on the left, or add a new element to the 'All Element' list (which will then be automatically used) or choose to leave the chosen HRE element as it is.

When you are satisfied with all of your choices, click on the 'Perform Conversion' button.

The conversion process will then implement your chosen elements into a new HRE Namestyle and delete the original TMG style.
