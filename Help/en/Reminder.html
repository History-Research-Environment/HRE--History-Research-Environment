Reminder Screen

The Reminder screen allows you to save information about how you make use of the HRE screen it is attached to, so you can refer to it later to maintain consistency or style of data you enter.

After you click the 'EDIT' button, you may enter freeform text, which can be edited by the use of the controls that appear at the top of the screen. These allow text to be formatted as bold, italic, underlined and/or as a list.

The 'SAVE' button saves the edited text.
