What do you want to do next?

At this point there are a number of tasks you may want to perform. A list of these and how to perform them is covered in the following paragraphs.

Setup HRE for your use: we strongly suggest you first go to 'Tools -> Settings -> User' to tailor HRE settings, particularly your preferred File Location settings.

To open the Sample project and explore it: in the Project menu, select 'Open Project', then select the Sample. Click on 'Open', or just double-click on the Sample entry.

To open any of your projects: if it is in the 'Recent Projects' list in the Project menu, just click on it. Otherwise select 'Open Project', then select your project. Click on 'Open', or just double-click on the required project entry.

To create a new empty project for your data: in the Project menu, select 'Create New Project' and follow the prompts to define its name and location.

To find and open a project given to you by someone else: in the Project menu, select 'Open Project', then click on 'Browse' to find the file you have been given. Then click on 'Open'.

To restore a previously backed up project: in the Project menu, select 'Restore Project' and follow the prompts.
